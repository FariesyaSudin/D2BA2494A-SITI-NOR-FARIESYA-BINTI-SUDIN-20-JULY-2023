Welcome to Asrama Bakti Anak Yatim Machang

Love, Unite and community.

The Asrama Bakti was built in 1987 and filled to capacity in 1988. In 1992, DYMM Raja Perempuan Kelantan gave it its official opening. The major reason for building this dormitory was to better and more comfortably support the education of orphans and underprivileged kids in Machang.Asrama Bakti's community is growing yearly. The number of residents was about 35 in 2015. They typically come from Machang, Kuala Krai, and Pasir Mas. They are entirely sponsored by the hostel and range in age from 7 to 17 years old. They attend schools close to Machang.
